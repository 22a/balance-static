Leveling Guide

Gunbreaker Levelling Rotations

Levels 15-29

(Solid Barrel at 26+)

> > Single target: (15-29) Keen Edge + Brutal Shell | (26-29) Solid Barrel ST combo

2 or more targets: Demon Slice spam

Levels 30-39

> Single target: Burst Strike > Solid Barrel ST combo

3 or more targets: Demon Slice spam

Levels 40-59

(Sonic Break at 54+)

> > Single target: Sonic Break > Burst Strike > Solid Barrel ST combo

> > 2 targets: Sonic Break > Burst Strike > Demon Slaughter AoE combo

> 3 or more targets: Sonic Break > Demon Slaughter AoE combo

Levels 60-71

> > > Single target: Sonic Break > Gnashing Fang GF combo > Burst Strike > Solid Barrel ST combo

> > > 2 targets: Sonic Break > Gnashing Fang GF combo > Burst Strike > Demon Slaughter AoE combo

> > 3 targets: Sonic Break > Gnashing Fang GF combo > Demon Slaughter AoE combo

> 4 or more targets: Sonic Break > Demon Slaughter AoE combo

From Level 60 onward, you should always save a Cartridge for Gnashing Fang instead of using it for Burst Strike/Fated Circle when Gnashing Fang has 10s or less on its cooldown. From Level 70 onward, Gnashing Fang, Savage Claw, and Wicked Talon should always be followed by their respective Continuation actions (Jugular Rip, Abdomen Tear, and Eye Gouge respectively).

Levels 72+

> > > Single target: Sonic Break > Gnashing Fang GF combo > Burst Strike > Solid Barrel ST combo

> > > 2 targets: Sonic Break > Gnashing Fang GF combo > Fated Circle > Demon Slaughter AoE combo

> > 4 or more targets: Sonic Break > Fated Circle > Demon Slaughter AoE combo
